How to prepare Tea

Description

Tea is an aromatic beverage prepared by pouring hot or boiling water over cured or fresh leaves of Camellia sinensis, an evergreen shrub native to East Asia which probably originated in the borderlands of southwestern China and northern Myanmar. Tea is also made, but rarely, from the leaves of Camellia taliensis.

Tea prepared from leaves that are processed differently can produce different types of tea. Like oxidising the leaves during the processing of leaves can leave tea leaves with different flavours to them. Today we will be preparing Black Tea.

Ingredients

Here is a list of ingredients required to prepare tea

  • Black Tea leaves
  • Herbs (optional)

How to make

Black Tea is prepared by the following steps

  1. First, boil some water over a kettle.
  2. Then, when prepare some tea leaves in a cup. 5 pieces of tea leaves for every 100 ml of water.
  3. And, pour the boiling water on top of the leaves.
  4. At last, add herbs or seasoning to your taste.

Now, you can enjoy your freshly prepared black tea.
